VaultX Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 26, 2026

VaultX ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how information is handled. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, usage, storage, and security when you use the VaultX application.

1. Scope of Policy

This Privacy Policy applies solely to the VaultX mobile application. By using the application, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information Collection

VaultX is designed with a privacy-first and zero-knowledge architecture.

We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data on external servers.

All user data, including but not limited to passwords, credentials, and notes, is stored locally on the user's device.

3. Zero-Knowledge Design

VaultX follows a zero-knowledge principle, meaning:

  • We have no access to your stored data
  • We cannot view, retrieve, or recover your information
  • All data remains fully under your control

4. Data Usage

All stored data is used strictly within the application to provide its core functionality. No data is transmitted outside the device.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties.

Since we do not collect data, there is no data available for disclosure to any entity.

6. Internet and Network Usage

VaultX does not require an internet connection for its primary features. Any optional network access (if present in future updates) will be clearly disclosed.

7. Data Security

VaultX implements industry-standard security principles, including:

  • Local data protection mechanisms
  • Secure storage practices
  • Device-level protection reliance

However, users are responsible for maintaining the security of their device, including screen locks and system protection.

8. Data Retention

All data remains on your device until you delete it manually or uninstall the application. We do not retain any copies externally.

9. User Control

You have full control over your data, including the ability to modify, delete, or reset stored information at any time.

10. Children's Privacy

VaultX does not knowingly collect or process any data from children under the age of 13.

11. Third-Party Services

VaultX does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

13. Legal Compliance

Although VaultX does not collect user data, we are committed to complying with applicable privacy laws and regulations where required.
